BMS INTEGRATION

With over 20 years of experience working in Buildings Management Systems, Controls Engineering, and Software Development & Integration we have a deep expertise in the development of custom solutions for BMS.

Tridium's Niagara N4 Framework is one of the most established and progressive platforms in BMS integration, and is an industry leader in edge computing, the Internet of Things(IoT) & connected devices. Despite it being the super-connectivity integration platform that it is, some projects may be unique in a way that N4 just doesn't quite cater for. That's where we can help.

Using Java to extend the Tridium Niagara Framework we can integrate anything with your BMS. From device drivers and Workbench features to integrating with your company's other enterprise software, or with cloud resources using APIs. Let us Build your own custom application on top of the Niagara framework, or modify, tweak and extend the functionality of N4 to meet your specific project needs.
